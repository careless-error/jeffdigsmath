I'm Jeff

Welcome to my site. Here’s a little about me, if you’re interested.

After graduating high school in 2007, I gave college a shot while working three jobs. For obvious reasons, it didn’t work out. I dropped out and spent the next stretch bouncing between gigs: sales, customer service, handyman work, security, logistics, warehouse operations—I even spent some time driving around in an old VW van, trying to figure out what was next.

In 2024, I finally went back to school. I’m now a full-time student studying math and computer science, earning straight As and actually enjoying it. Math came back quicker than I expected, and coding feels like math and writing smashed together in a way that clicks. I’ve taken a Python class, built a few math-related projects, and I’m learning Java now. A great English class even got me into writing. Tutoring reminded me how explaining something to someone else is one of the best ways to learn it yourself.

When I’m not at school or studying (see: procrastinating), I like to spend time outside and hang with my dogs. Indy is 14 and pretty much blind, but he’s still living his best life. Xena’s got some trauma and a bunch of triggers, but she tries to be a good girl. One of these days, I’ll take six months off and thru-hike the whole PCT. Outside of that, I’m pretty okay at bikes and just about anything mechanical (I used to be a car guy, but bikes are cheaper). I like figuring out how things work—bikes, cars, code, systems, people, my own brain...

This site is where I collect the things I’m working on: cheat sheets, code projects, tutoring tools, and anything else that feels worth keeping. It’s all a work in progress. So am I.
