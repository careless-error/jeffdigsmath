Riemann Sums & Area
Partition Width \( \Delta x \) = \( \frac{b-a}{n} \)
Subinterval Endpoint \( x_i \) = \( a + i \Delta x \)
Left Sum (Lower) \( s(n) \) = \( \sum\limits_{i=1}^{n} f(x_{i-1}) \Delta x \)
Right Sum (Upper) \( S(n) \) = \( \sum\limits_{i=1}^{n} f(x_i) \Delta x \)
Exact Area \( \lim\limits_{n\to\infty} \sum\limits_{i=1}^{n} f(x_i^*) \Delta x \)
