Factoring Techniques
Complete the Square \( x^2 + bx \) = \( \left(x + \frac{b}{2}\right)^2 - \frac{b^2}{4} \)
Difference of Squares \( a^2 - b^2 \) = \( (a - b)(a + b) \)
Perfect Squares \( a^2 \pm 2ab + b^2 \) = \( (a \pm b)^2 \)
Sum of Cubes \( a^3 \pm b^3 \) = \( (a \pm b)(a^2 \mp ab + b^2) \)
AC Method \( ax^2 + bx + c \) = \( (px + m)(qx + n) \)
where \( p \cdot q = a \), \( m \cdot n = c \),
and \( p \cdot n + q \cdot m = b \)
