Differentiation
Constant \( \frac{d}{dx}[c] \) = \( 0 \)
Power \( \frac{d}{dx}[x^n] \) = \( nx^{n-1} \)
Sum/Diff \( \frac{d}{dx}[f \pm g] \) = \( f' \pm g' \)
Product \( \frac{d}{dx}[fg] \) = \( f'g + fg' \)
Quotient \( \frac{d}{dx}\left[\frac{f}{g}\right] \) = \( \frac{f'g - fg'}{g^2} \)
Chain Rule \( \frac{d}{dx}[f(g(x))] \) = \( f'(g(x))g'(x) \)
Inverse Function \( (f^{-1})'(b) \) = \( \frac{1}{f'(a)} \quad \text{where } f(a) = b \)
Natural Log \( \frac{d}{dx}[\ln x] \) = \( \frac{1}{x} \)
Log Base \( a \) \( \frac{d}{dx}[\log_a x] \) = \( \frac{1}{x \ln a} \)
Exponential \( e^x \) \( \frac{d}{dx}[e^x] \) = \( e^x \)
Exponential \( a^x \) \( \frac{d}{dx}[a^x] \) = \( a^x \ln a \)
