Algebraic Limit Techniques
Factor and cancel \( \frac{x^2 - 4}{x - 2} = \frac{(x - 2)(x + 2)}{x - 2} \rightarrow x + 2 \)
hole at \( x = 2 \)
Multiply by conjugate \( \frac{\sqrt{1 + x} - 1}{x} \cdot \frac{\sqrt{1 + x} + 1}{\sqrt{1 + x} + 1} = \frac{x}{x(\sqrt{1 + x} + 1)} \)
use when radicals prevent substitution
Common denominator \( \frac{1}{x} - \frac{1}{x + h} = \frac{h}{x(x + h)} \)
used when combining rational expressions
Clear complex fractions \( \frac{\frac{1}{h}}{\frac{1}{h} + 1} = \frac{1}{1 + h} \)
multiply top and bottom by LCD to eliminate inner fractions
