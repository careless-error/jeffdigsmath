Inverse Trig & Log Integrals
Arcsin \( \displaystyle \int \frac{1}{\sqrt{a^2 - x^2}}\,dx \) = \( \displaystyle \arcsin\left(\frac{x}{a}\right) + C \)
Arctan \( \displaystyle \int \frac{1}{a^2 + x^2}\,dx \) = \( \displaystyle \frac{1}{a} \arctan\left(\frac{x}{a}\right) + C \)
Arcsec \( \displaystyle \int \frac{1}{x\sqrt{x^2 - a^2}}\,dx \) = \( \displaystyle \frac{1}{a}\,\text{arcsec}\!\bigl(\tfrac{|x|}{a}\bigr) + C \)
General Log \( \displaystyle \int \frac{f'(x)}{f(x)}\,dx \) = \( \displaystyle \ln|f(x)| + C \)
Tangent \( \displaystyle \int \tan x\,dx \) = \( \displaystyle -\ln|\cos x| + C \)
Secant \( \displaystyle \int \sec x\,dx \) = \( \displaystyle \ln|\sec x + \tan x| + C \)
