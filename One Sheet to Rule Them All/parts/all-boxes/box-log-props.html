Logarithm Properties
\( \ln(1) \) = \( 0 \)
\( \ln(e) \) = \( 1 \)
\( \ln(x^n) \) = \( n \ln(x) \)
\( \ln(xy) \) = \( \ln(x) + \ln(y) \)
\( \ln\left(\frac{x}{y}\right) \) = \( \ln(x) - \ln(y) \)
\( \ln(e^x) \) = \( x \)
\( \log_a(x) \) = \( \frac{\ln(x)}{\ln(a)} \)
\( \ln(x) \) undefined for \( x < 0 \)
